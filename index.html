Statistical Consultancy for Healthcare Professionals

Welcome to Metrapi

Metrapi provides professional statistical support for research projects in healthcare, specializing in helping healthcare workers in Ireland achieve their research goals. Whether it's analyzing data, designing studies, or interpreting results, Metrapi ensures your research is robust and adheres to scientific best practices.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services to meet your research and data needs:

  • Study Protocol Design – Create structured and methodologically sound research plans.
  • Study Analysis – Perform advanced statistical tests and interpret results accurately.
  • Data Cleaning and Wrangling – Ensure high-quality, reliable datasets before analysis.
  • Predictive Modelling and Machine Learning – Generate actionable insights using cutting-edge ML techniques.
  • eCRF, Database Design, and Data Management – Develop efficient data capture systems and maintain data integrity.
  • Grant and Publication Review – Strengthen funding proposals and manuscripts for peer-reviewed journals.
